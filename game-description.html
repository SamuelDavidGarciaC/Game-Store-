    Product Description

    We welcome you to Yara, a tropical paradise that has stopped in time.
    Yara dictator Antón Castillo is determined to return his nation to its former lost glory by any means
    and his son Diego follows in his bloody footsteps. The oppression to which they submit their people has sparked a revolution.

    - Play as Dani Rojas, a resident of Yara who participates in a current guerrilla revolution to liberate the country!
    - Take on Castillo's regime in the biggest Far Cry to date, roaming through jungles, beaches, and Esperanza, the capital of Yara.
    - Employ an arsenal of Resolver weapons, backpacks, and vehicles to topple Castillo's ruthless regime.

    Features of Far Cry 6:

    • Open world tropical environment with different forms of wildlife, multiple factions and urban areas such as the capital of Esperanza;
    • Far Cry 6 introduces third-person scenes for the first time in the series for a more cinematic experience;
    • Players will be able to choose the gender of the main character Dani Rojas;
    • Far Cry fans once again have a memorable villain, this time its president Antón Castillo, played by Giancarlo Esposito;
